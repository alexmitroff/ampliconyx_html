About

The Company

Ampliconyx Oy is a Tampere based spin-off company from Optoelectronics Research Centre of Tampere University of Technology (www.tut.fi/orc) – European leader in photonics technology. Thanks to its proprietary IP, Ampliconyx offers unmatched cost/performance in ultrafast all-fiber lasers and amplifiers for major industries with tremendous market potential based on wide range of applications.

The Team and Experience

Ampliconyx core team comprises highly experienced professionals with prominent track in optoelectronics, fiber laser technology, optoelectronic system engineering and business development. Team know-how includes system design as well as manufacturing technology of key component and allows to design and manufacturing of broad range of fiber optical devices, including powerful supercontinuum generators - “white lasers”, high power femtosecond lasers, LIDARs, mid infrared lasers and others.

The company management team has over 10 years’ experience in managing start-up companies, product development, sales and marketing, transition to mass production phase and high volume production management. Track records includes exits for investors with multiple returns.
